WHY ATTEND?

1. Get away from school

Whether you started in August or September, every student needs to get away from studying and just take a break sometime in the semester. What better way to get away from school than with your Circle K family?! FTC is a great weekend away to (temporarily) escape lectures, quizzes and midterms.

2. Meet new people

From Nevada to San Diego, Hawai'i to San Francisco, you will get the chance to meet people from all over our district! During game time, you'll be teamed up with people from schools all over California, Nevada and Hawai'i, so you'll get a lot of chances to make new friends!

3. See the stars

Fall Training Conference is going to be held at Old Oak Ranch up in the mountains of Sonora, and when you get further away from the city lights, the first thing you'll be able to see when you get up the mountain on Friday evening is stars! Take some time and lie down under the stars one night during the weekend and enjoy nature's beauty.

4. Get officially inducted

As a new member, you will be able to take part in the District-wide New Member Installation! You'll get a certificate of membership and go through a ceremony rooted in tradition

5. Show your spirit

Whether it be waiting for opening session to start, at the spirit workshop, or against another division, be ready to bring your spirit! All throughout FTC you will hear club cheers and divisional cheers being thrown at each other, but that doesn't always mean we are at it to beat everyone else - feel free to join in any other club or division's cheers too!

6. Gain knowledge from workshops

If you want to learn about the basics of Circle K, get to know more about the different leadership positions at the club and district levels, or just learn a few cheers, FTC is the place for you! FTC has hosted workshops like Battle of the Sexes, CKI 101 and even more coming this year!

7. Dance your heart away

To celebrate the end of the second day of FTC, you are welcome to participate in all the activities that lead into Saturday night, such as a dance and a live DJ in the main auditorium. In the past years there have been henna tattoos, video games and board games, too!

8. Showcase your talent

Every year we all come to the fire pit to watch a few skits put on by clubs, and individual talent acts! YOU can be a part of the talent too! All talent acts and campfire skits have an application process and deadlines you can find here.

Does this sound exciting to you? Contact your club president to find out how to attend!
