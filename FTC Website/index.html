SAA FORM

What is a Sergeant-At-Arms?

Sergeant-At-Arms (SAAs) are amazing volunteers who are willing to take an active part in helping the district. They make sure that FTC goes on without a hitch by promoting and enforcing safe behavior as listed in the Code of Conduct. Their responsibilities include making rounds around the campground, reporting inappropriate activities, and making sure that each member has a safe experience. Our FTC ohana would be incomplete without these members!

  • SAA selection notification: October 30th
  • SAA training
  • Webinar (November 1st, November 2nd), SAAs should attend one webinar
  • Onsite (November 4th and 5th)

TEAM CAPTAIN APPLICATION
Team Captain Duties

Team Captains should be familiarized with all the games they will be participating in and leading prior to attending Fall Training Conference. As a Team Captain, you will be leading your team of Circle K members (roughly 15, consisting of new and older members) throughout a series of games and challenges presented during the course of the day. Your job is not only to facilitate and encourage bonding between your team members as you participate in games and overcome challenges presented to, but most importantly to have fun!

How many attendees can I bring to FTC?

Each school will be guaranteed space for 20 attendees. Further members wishing to attend will be placed on a waitlist.

Are meals included in the price?

Yes, attendees will be provided three meals on Saturday and breakfast on Sunday.

What happens if I arrive late to FTC?

Registration closes at 12AM, but will reopen the next day at 6AM. You will be able to find housing locations posted on the door to the lobby.

How should I pack for FTC?

Keep in mind that the weather in Sonora will be cold! Stay tuned as we will be releasing a packing list as the event approaches.

How much does FTC cost?

Early Registration, due by October 14th will cost $143 per member. After that date, the registration price will rise to $168 per member. Registration closes October on 21st.

How many SAAs does my club need?

Each club will be expected to register a minimum of 2 SAAs, plus an additional SAA for every four members that attend. Review the SAA form for more detailed information.

Can my club sell merchandise/fundraise at FTC?

Yes, on Saturday night.
